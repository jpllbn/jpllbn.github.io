I. Introduction

About the Tour

The Educational Tour was conducted on December 9-13, 2025, visiting various IT companies and institutions in Cebu City, Mandaue City & Lapu-Lapu City. This immersive experience was designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world industry practices.

Purpose

The tour aimed to expose students to the actual working environment of IT professionals, understand industry standards, and gain insights into emerging technologies and career opportunities in the field of Information Technology.

Significance to ITE 101

As part of the ITE 101 curriculum, this educational tour reinforces classroom learning by providing hands-on exposure to industry practices. It allows students to witness how fundamental IT concepts are applied in professional settings, from software development methodologies to network infrastructure management.

II. Objectives

01

Industry Exposure

To provide students with firsthand experience of the IT industry's working environment and professional culture.

02

Technology Awareness

To familiarize students with current technologies, tools, and methodologies used in professional IT settings.

03

Career Guidance

To help students identify potential career paths and understand the skills required for various IT roles.

04

Networking Opportunities

To establish connections with industry professionals and potential employers for future internship and job opportunities.

05

Practical Application

To relate theoretical concepts learned in ITE 101 to real-world applications and industry best practices.

III. Tour Narrative

Day 1

December 9, 2025 (Tuesday) - Assembly & Departure

We assembled at SAC Grounds at 12:00 PM. At 1:00 PM, we proceeded to Iloilo City, arriving at 4:00 PM. At 7:00 PM, we departed for Cebu City via ferry. Lights off and rest at 9:00 PM.

Day 2

December 10, 2025 (Wednesday) - Cebu City Visits

We arrived in Cebu City at 7:00 AM and had breakfast at 8:00 AM.

White Brick Creative Studio

Visit 1 - 9:30 AM

Company Background

White Brick Creative Studio is located at 310 Mango Square, Gen. Maxilom Ave., Cebu City. They specialize in visual communication and digital brand promotion.

Activities

Orientation on visual communication and digital brand promotion. We learned about creative design processes and branding strategies.

Insights Gained

Understanding the importance of visual identity in the digital age and how creative studios help businesses establish their brand presence.

Worldtech Information Solutions

Visit 2 - 1:30 PM

Company Background

Located at Vailoces Bldg., Rahmann St., Gorordo Ave., Kamputhaw, Cebu City. Worldtech specializes in enterprise solutions, cybersecurity, and networking.

Activities

Orientation on enterprise solutions, cybersecurity and networking. We learned about protecting digital assets and network infrastructure management.

Insights Gained

The critical importance of cybersecurity in today's connected world and how enterprise solutions integrate various IT systems.

Fizzbuzz, Inc.

Visit 3

Company Background

Located at Meridian Bldg., Golam Drive, Cebu City. Fizzbuzz specializes in stop-motion animation and creative digital content.

Activities

Orientation on Stop-Motion Animation. We explored creative animation techniques and digital storytelling methods.

Insights Gained

Animation and creative content creation are vital components of modern digital media, combining technical skills with artistic vision.

Day 3

December 11, 2025 (Thursday) - Mandaue & Lapu-Lapu City

Wake-up call at 6:00 AM, breakfast at 7:00 AM, then proceeded to company visits at 8:00 AM.

Mandaue Command Center

Visit 4 - 9:30 AM

Company Background

Located in Mandaue City, Cebu. The Command Center is responsible for disaster and accident response coordination for the city.

Activities

Orientation on disaster and accident response. We observed how ICT systems are used in emergency management and public safety.

Insights Gained

Technology plays a crucial role in emergency response and public safety, enabling faster coordination and better resource management.

MyTV Cebu

Visit 5 - 1:00 PM

Company Background

Located at 1 Salinas Dr., Lahug, Cebu City. MyTV Cebu is a broadcasting company specializing in video streaming and digital broadcasting.

Activities

Orientation on Video Streaming and Digital Broadcasting. We learned about modern broadcasting technologies and content delivery systems.

Insights Gained

The evolution of broadcasting from traditional TV to digital streaming platforms demonstrates the rapid transformation of media technology.

PAGASA Cebu

Agency Visit - 3:00 PM

Company Background

Located at Airport Rd., Lapu-Lapu City. PAGASA (Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration) is the national meteorological agency.

Activities

Orientation on ICT in Weather Forecasting. We learned how technology is used in weather prediction and climate monitoring.

Insights Gained

Advanced ICT systems are essential for accurate weather forecasting, which impacts agriculture, disaster preparedness, and daily life.

Day 4

December 12, 2025 (Friday) - City Tour & Departure

Wake-up call at 6:00 AM, breakfast at hotel at 7:00 AM, then check-out.

Cebu City Tour

Cultural Tour - 1:00 PM

Places Visited

Basilica Minore del Sto. Niño, Magellan's Cross, Cebu City Museum, and Pasalubong Centers.

Activities

Cultural and historical tour of Cebu City's famous landmarks, learning about the rich heritage of the Queen City of the South.

Insights Gained

Understanding local culture and history enriches our perspective as IT professionals working in diverse communities.

Day 5

December 13, 2025 (Saturday) - Return Home

Arrived in Iloilo City at 7:00 AM. Proceeded to Antique at 8:00 AM. Arrived HOME at 11:00 AM. End of the educational tour.

V. Reflection

Personal Reflection Essay

The educational tour to Cebu City, Mandaue City, and Lapu-Lapu City from December 9-13, 2025, was a transformative experience that significantly shaped my perspective on the IT industry and my future career path. Before this trip, my understanding of the professional IT world was limited to what I learned in textbooks and classroom discussions. However, witnessing firsthand how theoretical concepts translate into real-world applications was truly enlightening.

At White Brick Creative Studio, I was impressed by how visual communication and digital branding come together to create impactful brand identities. The creative processes they shared showed me that IT isn't just about coding—it's also about design thinking and understanding how technology can tell compelling stories for businesses.

The visit to Worldtech Information Solutions opened my eyes to the critical importance of cybersecurity and enterprise solutions. Learning about how they protect digital assets and manage network infrastructure reinforced what we study in ITE 101 about the responsibility that comes with handling sensitive information in today's connected world.

Perhaps the most unique experience was at Fizzbuzz Inc., where we explored stop-motion animation. This visit demonstrated that IT careers extend far beyond traditional programming—creative digital content creation combines technical skills with artistic vision in fascinating ways.

The Mandaue Command Center showed me how ICT plays a vital role in public safety and emergency response. Seeing real-time disaster coordination systems in action gave me a deeper appreciation for how technology can save lives. Similarly, at PAGASA Cebu, I learned how advanced ICT systems enable accurate weather forecasting that impacts agriculture, disaster preparedness, and daily life across the country.

At MyTV Cebu, the evolution from traditional broadcasting to digital streaming platforms demonstrated the rapid transformation of media technology—a reminder that as IT professionals, we must continuously adapt to changing landscapes.

This tour has solidified my commitment to pursuing a career in Information Technology. I now have clearer goals: to excel in my studies, develop both technical and soft skills, and eventually contribute meaningfully to the industry. The connections I made and the insights I gained from these Cebu-based companies will undoubtedly guide my academic and professional journey moving forward.

VI. Key Learnings & Outcomes

Technical Skills Matter

Proficiency in programming, networking, and database management are fundamental requirements in the industry.

Soft Skills Are Essential

Communication, teamwork, and adaptability are equally valued by employers alongside technical expertise.

Continuous Learning

The tech industry evolves rapidly; professionals must commit to lifelong learning to stay relevant.

Security is Paramount

Cybersecurity is a critical concern across all IT sectors, from data centers to software development.

Innovation Drives Growth

Companies that embrace innovation and creativity are more likely to succeed in the competitive tech landscape.

Global Opportunities

The IT industry offers diverse career paths with opportunities for international exposure and growth.
